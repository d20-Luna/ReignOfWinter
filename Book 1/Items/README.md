# New Items - The Snows of Summer

New traits will be indicated with a carat (^) and are linked to their explanation.

## Cloak of the Yeti

**Level** 3

**Usage** worn cloak; **Bulk** 1

This heavy cloak is crafted from shaggy white yeti fur. When the cloak’s hood is pulled up over the wearer’s head, it takes on a semblance of its arctic namesake’s fearsome visage. Northern warriors value its ability to ward against the chill of winter, as well as the protection it provides in combat. The cloak of the yeti provides a constant *elemental endurance* effect in cold weather (it has no effect in hot weather). In battle, the cloak wraps around the wearer, granting a +1 status bonus to AC. In addition, with the hood drawn over the wearer’s head, the cloak grants a +1 item bonus on Intimidation checks.
